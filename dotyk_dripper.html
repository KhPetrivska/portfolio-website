DOTYK DRIPPER

WEB DESIGN PROJECT

Year:
2023

Technologies:
Figma

Summary:
A website for Dotyk Dripper, the first Ukrainian company dedicated to crafting accessories for coffee brewing, with a primary focus on pour-over equipment. Company founders produce a device made with new unique technology for coffee lovers, crafted from organic materials, while adeptly weaving Ukraine's cultural code into the branding concept. Similarly, this web design project seamlessly blends traditional Ukrainian culture with contemporary and minimalist design elements, meticulously reflecting the brand's essence.
